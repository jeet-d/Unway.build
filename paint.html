Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Nerolac wall paints unique?
Nerolac uses advanced formulas and technology to create paints with vibrant, long-lasting colours and smooth finishes that protect walls. The paints are designed to beautify and safeguard.
How does Nerolac ensure eco-friendliness in its products?
Nerolac paints are formulated with low VOCs, no lead, and reduced emissions to minimize environmental impact and create a healthier home.
What are Nerolac’s popular products for interior painting?
Popular interior paint options are Impressions Ultra HD, Luxury Silk, and Velvet Touch. These provide stunning finishes, easy maintenance, and protection from stains and microbes.
How can I choose the best paint finish for my home?
Consult Nerolac's colour experts to determine which finish option, such as matte or high-gloss, best suits your decor style, lighting, and functional needs.
Does Nerolac offer specialized services for home painting?
Yes, Nerolac provides end-to-end painting services including waterproofing, surface prep, colour consultation, and professional paint application for a hassle-free experience.
What’s the difference between emulsion and enamel paints?
Emulsion paints are water-based, ideal for walls, and offer smooth finishes and washability, while enamel paints are oil-based, extremely durable, and perfect for wood and metal.
Can Nerolac paints be used for creative wall designs?
Yes, Nerolac offers textured and decorative paints to create unique finishes, patterns, stencils, and more to make stunning design focal points.
Does Nerolac offer solutions for exterior painting?
Yes, Nerolac exterior paints like Excel Anti-Peel and Excel Everlast withstand weather, resist algae, and maintain vibrant colours for years.
Are Nerolac paints suitable for high-moisture areas?
Yes, Nerolac’s moisture-resistant paints are specifically designed for areas prone to humidity, such as kitchens and bathrooms. These paints prevent peeling, staining, and mold growth.
What are the latest trends in wall painting designs?
Nerolac offers a range of trendy options, from pastel palettes to bold textures and geometric patterns. Explore our catalogue for the latest ideas and inspiration to elevate your walls.
How can I get expert advice on colour combinations?
Nerolac offers professional colour consultation services to help you select the perfect shades and combinations. Our digital tools, like the Nerolac Color Visualizer
